Optimasi Kinerja Analisis Kernel Support Vector Machine (SVM) Untuk Klasifikasi Operasi Caesar Persalinan

June 2025

This project focuses on optimizing the performance of the Support Vector Machine (SVM) algorithm in classifying cesarean section operations based on medical and maternal data. The main objective is to enhance the model’s accuracy and efficiency by analyzing and comparing different kernel functions, such as linear, polynomial, and radial basis function (RBF) kernels. Through a series of experiments and parameter tuning, the project aims to identify the most effective kernel configuration for predicting the likelihood of a cesarean delivery. The results provide valuable insights for improving decision-making processes in healthcare data analysis, particularly in the field of maternal health and medical prediction systems.
